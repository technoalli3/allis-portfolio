About Commissions:

As of right now, I do not have a set-in-stone commissions table. However, I believe in fair pricing. Before any work is done, we can discuss what a fair price may be. The final price will never stray more than $100.00 from the original estimated price, and any estimated changes will be communicated. Pricing bounds will vary based on the requested commission, and I will communicate those bounds.

In all honesty, accepting commissions from customers isn't about the money. I love modeling and texturing things, and I do this for the experience more than anything.

Below is a list of my current capabilities. This may change and update as I gain experience through commissions.

VRChat

  • Worlds:
    • Custom props
    • Basic functionality (doors, toggles, etc)
    • Custom textures (to a degree; I don't have much experience in creating textures from scratch)
  • Avatars:
    • Rigging/setup of existing model
    • Addition of props, accessories, clothing, or other similar additions
    • Retexturing (note: I am not very experienced in creation of textures from scratch, however I can modify existing textures)
    • Minor modifications to meshes (facial details, position/rotation of limbs, physique changes)
    • Installation/setup of prefabs or scripts (example being GoGo Loco)
